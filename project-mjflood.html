Brand & Digital

MJ Flood

A complete digital transformation for Ireland's largest indigenous supplier of office technology, managed print services, security systems, and coffee solutions — unifying 90 years of heritage under a bold new digital presence.

The Challenge

Founded in 1935 as a typewriter sales and service company, MJ Flood has evolved into a multi-division technology group with four distinct business units: Printing & Imaging, Managed Print Services, Security Systems, and Coffee Solutions. With eight regional branches stretching from Belfast to Waterford, the group serves thousands of businesses across Ireland.

However, their digital presence hadn't kept pace with this evolution. Each division operated with fragmented branding and disconnected websites, creating confusion for clients and undermining the group's position as a unified market leader. They needed a cohesive digital ecosystem that told the story of a modern, forward-thinking company while honouring their nine decades of heritage.

Our Approach

We began with a comprehensive discovery phase, interviewing stakeholders across all four divisions, auditing competitor landscapes, and conducting user research with existing and prospective clients.

  • Stakeholder workshops across Dublin HQ and regional branches
  • Competitive audit of technology providers across Ireland and the UK
  • User journey mapping for each division's primary audience segments
  • Brand architecture strategy to unify four divisions under one umbrella
  • Content strategy aligning messaging across print, security, and coffee verticals

The strategic insight was clear: MJ Flood's strength lies in being a single trusted partner across multiple technology needs. The digital experience needed to make this cross-selling opportunity effortless while giving each division room to breathe.

Brand Identity

The refreshed visual identity retains the equity of the MJ Flood name while introducing a modern, modular system. A new brandmark works as both a unified group logo and a flexible framework for each division — Printing & Imaging, Managed Print, Security, and Coffee Solutions — each receiving a distinct colour accent within the shared system.

Typography was updated to a contemporary geometric sans-serif, paired with a confident colour palette that transitions from corporate authority to approachable expertise depending on the division context. Photography direction moved away from generic stock towards authentic imagery shot across their Dublin headquarters and regional operations.

Digital Experience

The new website consolidates four previously separate sites into a single, intelligent platform. Users can navigate seamlessly between divisions while a smart recommendation engine surfaces relevant cross-division services based on browsing behaviour.

Key UX decisions included a branch finder with real-time availability, self-service quote builders for print and security solutions, and a resource hub positioning MJ Flood as thought leaders in workplace technology. The site was built headless on a modern JAMstack architecture, delivering sub-second page loads and a perfect Lighthouse score.

Studio understood our business from day one. They took a complex, multi-division organisation and created a digital presence that finally tells our full story. The results have exceeded every benchmark we set.
— Ronan Flood, Managing Director, MJ Flood Group

The Results

Within six months of launch, MJ Flood saw a 200% increase in organic traffic, a 340% improvement in online enquiry volume, and measurable cross-division discovery — with 28% of users exploring services outside their initial entry point. The unified brand has strengthened their position in enterprise procurement conversations, and the digital platform now serves as the cornerstone of their growth strategy.
