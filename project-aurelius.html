The Challenge

Aurelius Finance set out to challenge legacy banking with a mobile-first approach to personal wealth management. Their technology was solid — algorithmic portfolio management, real-time market data, zero-commission trading — but their existing app felt like a spreadsheet with a login screen.

They needed design that could make finance feel approachable without sacrificing the sophistication that high-net-worth users expect. Trust, clarity, and delight in equal measure.

Our Approach

  • 120+ user interviews across three European markets
  • Competitive UX audit of 15 fintech and traditional banking apps
  • Design sprint methodology with weekly prototype testing
  • Accessibility-first approach exceeding WCAG 2.1 AA standards
  • Comprehensive design system with 200+ components

Design Language

We created a visual language built on three principles: calm confidence, progressive disclosure, and purposeful motion. The dark-mode-first interface uses a restrained palette of navy, white, and a single accent green that signals growth and positive action. Complex data is revealed in layers, letting users drill down without feeling overwhelmed.

Intelligent Interactions

Every interaction was designed to build confidence. Portfolio performance is communicated through subtle animations that make gains feel celebratory without trivialising losses. Haptic feedback reinforces key actions like trades and transfers. Biometric authentication keeps security invisible, and smart notifications surface insights without creating anxiety.

Studio turned our technology into an experience people love using. Our retention rates are the highest in the European fintech space, and the design is a major reason why.
— Dr. Lukas Weber, CEO, Aurelius Finance

The Results

The redesigned app drove user acquisition to 2M+ within 18 months, with a 62% daily active rate that outperforms industry benchmarks by 3x. App Store and Google Play ratings sit at 4.9, and the design system we built now powers their entire product ecosystem including web and tablet experiences.
