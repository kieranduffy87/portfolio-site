Digital Campaign

Voss Architecture

Establishing an international digital presence for a Scandinavian architecture firm through a multi-channel campaign combining stunning project photography, editorial content, and targeted social strategy.

Client
Voss Architecture
Services
Digital Campaign, Web Design, Content Strategy, Social Media
Year
2024
Location
Copenhagen, Denmark

The Challenge

Voss Architecture is one of Scandinavia's most respected studios, known for residential and cultural projects that blend minimalist form with deep contextual sensitivity. Despite winning multiple national awards, their international profile was virtually non-existent. Potential clients outside the Nordics had never heard of them.

They needed a digital strategy that would position them on the global stage — attracting international commissions while staying true to their understated Scandinavian ethos.

Our Approach

  • Complete website redesign as a visual portfolio and editorial platform
  • Commissioned photography of 18 completed projects across four countries
  • Content strategy spanning long-form editorial, social media, and press
  • Targeted LinkedIn and Instagram campaigns reaching architecture decision-makers
  • SEO strategy focused on international architectural search terms

Visual Storytelling

The new website presents each project as an immersive narrative — not just photographs, but the story of the site, the brief, the constraints, and the creative decisions that shaped the final form. Full-bleed imagery, subtle scroll-driven animations, and minimal text create a gallery-like experience that lets the architecture speak.

Content & Social Strategy

We developed a content calendar built around four pillars: Project Stories, Process Insights, Material Studies, and Nordic Perspectives. Each piece was crafted for dual distribution — working as standalone editorial on the site and adapted for high-impact social posts. A targeted LinkedIn campaign reaching architectural decision-makers at developers, cultural institutions, and municipal planners delivered qualified leads from eight new international markets.

Studio understood that architecture firms need to show, not tell. They created a digital presence that feels as considered as our buildings. Within a year, we had commissions from three countries where we'd never worked before.
— Henrik Voss, Founding Partner, Voss Architecture

The Results

Within 12 months, Voss Architecture's social following grew 500%, with their Instagram becoming a reference point in the architecture community. Inbound enquiries tripled, with international leads now comprising 40% of their pipeline. The website won an Awwwards Honorable Mention, and the campaign as a whole picked up 12 industry design awards.

Aurelius Finance MJ Flood
