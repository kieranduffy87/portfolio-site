Web Design

Meridian Hotels

An immersive digital booking experience for a boutique hotel group with 12 properties across Europe — transforming browsers into guests through emotional storytelling and frictionless UX.

Client
Meridian Hotels Group
Services
Web Design, UX/UI, Booking System, Digital Strategy
Year
2024
Location
London, UK

The Challenge

Meridian Hotels operates 12 boutique properties across six European countries. Despite stunning physical spaces and a loyal guest base, their online conversion rate lagged far behind industry benchmarks. The existing website felt transactional rather than aspirational — it showed rooms and prices but failed to convey the unique character of each property.

They needed a digital experience that matched the quality of their hospitality — one that made guests feel the warmth of a Meridian stay before they ever walked through the door.

Our Approach

We conducted on-site research at four key properties, observing guest interactions and interviewing staff to understand what makes a Meridian stay distinctive. The insight: guests don't book rooms, they book feelings.

  • On-site ethnographic research across four flagship properties
  • Guest journey mapping from discovery to post-stay advocacy
  • Bespoke photography and videography direction for each property
  • Custom booking engine with real-time availability and dynamic pricing

Immersive Storytelling

Each property page opens with a cinematic full-screen video that captures the essence of the location — the morning light over Lake Como, the buzz of a Barcelona rooftop bar, the quiet elegance of a Cotswolds manor. Users scroll through curated narratives before reaching availability, building desire before asking for commitment.

Booking Experience

The custom booking engine integrates seamlessly with their property management system while maintaining the editorial feel of the site. Room selection uses immersive imagery rather than dropdown menus, and the checkout flow was reduced from seven steps to three — cutting abandonment by 62%.

Studio didn't just build us a website — they created a digital extension of our hospitality. Guests tell us they felt excited before they even arrived. That's exactly what we wanted.
— Alexander Novak, CEO, Meridian Hotels Group

The Results

Direct online bookings increased 180% in the first quarter post-launch, with a 45% reduction in bounce rate and average session duration nearly tripling. The site won a CSS Design Award and was featured on Awwwards as Site of the Day.
