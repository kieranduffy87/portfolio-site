Noir Collective

Building a premium direct-to-consumer fashion platform from scratch — where editorial storytelling meets seamless commerce and every interaction reinforces the brand's quiet luxury positioning.

Client
Noir Collective
Services
E-Commerce, Brand Identity, Digital Marketing, Photography
Year
2023
Location
Paris, France

The Challenge

Noir Collective is a Parisian fashion house specialising in elevated essentials — pieces designed to be worn every day but crafted with couture-level attention to detail. After years of wholesale-only distribution, they were ready to launch direct-to-consumer, but needed a digital storefront that matched the calibre of their product.

The challenge: build a luxury e-commerce experience that doesn't feel like an e-commerce site. Every touchpoint needed to reinforce the brand's philosophy of understated sophistication.

Our Approach

  • Brand identity evolution from wholesale to consumer-facing positioning
  • Editorial-first e-commerce UX that prioritises storytelling over catalogue browsing
  • Bespoke photography direction spanning lookbooks, product, and lifestyle
  • Headless Shopify architecture for performance and flexibility
  • Integrated digital marketing strategy across paid and organic channels

Editorial Commerce

The site blurs the line between magazine and store. Each collection launches with a dedicated editorial spread featuring commissioned photography and art direction. Products are contextualised within stories rather than isolated on grid pages, creating an emotional connection before the purchasing decision.

Seamless Checkout

The purchasing journey was designed to be invisible — reducing friction to the absolute minimum. A persistent mini-cart, Apple Pay integration, and a two-step checkout resulted in an abandonment rate 40% below industry average. Personalised recommendations drive a 32% increase in average order value.

They built more than a store — they built a world. Our customers don't just shop with us, they spend time with us. That distinction has transformed our business.
— Camille Deschamps, Founder, Noir Collective

The Results

The DTC launch exceeded projections by 280%, achieving profitability in the first quarter. Average order value sits 45% above initial targets, and the site's editorial approach has driven significant organic growth, with fashion press coverage generating over 2M impressions in the first six months.
